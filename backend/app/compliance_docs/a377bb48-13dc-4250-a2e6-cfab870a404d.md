# Content Compliance Guidelines

1. Clear , Plain Language Only
All content must be written in simple, accessible language. Avoid jar gon unless absolutely
necessary—and explain it when used.
2. Accuracy is Non-Negotiable
Every piece of content must be fact-checked and up to date. Outdated or misleading information
must be corrected immediately .
3. Transpar ency at All Times
Disclose terms, conditions, risks, and limitations clearly . Important details should never be hidden
in fine print.
4. Customer -Centric Tone
Write with empathy and relevance. Content should address real customer needs, not internal
priorities.
5. Consistent Brand Voice
Maintain a tone that is professional, calm, and trustworthy . Avoid slang, exaggeration, or overly
casual phrasing.
6. Compliance First
All content must align with legal, regulatory , and internal policy requirements before publication.
7. Inclusive and Respectful Language
Ensure content is free from bias, discrimination, or exclusion. It should be accessible and
respectful to all audiences.
8. Purpose-Driven Content
Every piece of content must have a clear goal—inform, guide, support, or resolve. Avoid
unnecessary or filler content.
9. Data Privacy Awareness
Do not include or expose sensitive customer data. Use anonymized or fictional examples where
needed.
10. Review and Accountability
All content must go through a review process. Ownership should be clearly defined for creation,
approval, and updates.
